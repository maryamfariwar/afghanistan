KABUL- Capital Of AFG!

Kabul is the capital and largest city of Afghanistan, situated in a mountain valley at approximately 1,800 meters above sea level.
With a population of over 4 million,
it serves as the country’s political, cultural, and economic center.
Kabul has a rich history that spans over 3,500 years, >having been part of major empires including the Achaemenids, Kushans, and Mughals.
Once known for its vibrant culture and strategic importance along ancient trade routes, the city has endured decades of conflict,
particularly during the civil war of the 1990s and the U.S.-led war after 2001. It fell under Taliban control in August 2021, marking a major shift in governance and daily life.
Today, Kabul remains a city of resilience, home to historical sites like Babur’s Gardens and the National Museum,
even as it grapples with economic hardship, security concerns, and social restrictions.
